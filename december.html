Happy Christmas and New Year to all of our Members

and a HUGE Thank you to Angela and Sheila for all their hard work running this grouponce again in an adverse year.

Reindeer were rounded up from long Marston residents and herded to the village hall to join Ruben the reindeer for Long Marston Ladies Christmas Party and 10 year celebration.

This years theme was Winter Wonderland all tables had snowflakes and of course reindeer.

The stage sparkled with snow and hundreds of pine cones. Isobel Slatter once again catered for almost 50 ladies, starting with mushrooms or prawn cocktail and a main of traditional Christmas dinner, polished off with either Christmas pud or Baileys cheesecake.

Dancing queen then played getting ladies on their feet to dance the night away.

Angela was presented with a beautiful orchid and a voucher from everyone in the group.

Long Marston Ladies restart in February 2022 with a full interesting programme. In the mean time we wish everyone a very Happy Christmas and a wonderful New Year.
