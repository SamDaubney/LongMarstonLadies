Alan Brewer - Up in the Clouds

with Long Marston Ladies

Alan Brewer

Up in the Clouds

In June the Long Marston Ladies were “Up in the Clouds” with their guest speaker Alan Brewer.

Alan had a fascinating story about how he started cooking for Fanny Craddock and The Queen after joining BOAC back in the 1970’s. His stories about the Queen were brilliant and how she came across as a lovely lady.

Once he became Purser on long haul flights he met Joan Collins, Adam Faith, Cliff Richard, Sophia Loren and Richard Gere - to name a few. Each one came with a story!
He also looked after Tony Blair for nine years and travelled the globe with him.

He has travelled the world several times over and all from the humble beginnings of being a pastry chef.
