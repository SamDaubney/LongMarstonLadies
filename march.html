Chair Yoga

with Sara Sharma

Chair Yoga

Sara Sharma

A very rare event happened at the March meeting of Long Marston Ladies. Almost an hour of quiet as Sara Sharma's soothing calm voice gave directions on the moves for chair yoga.

The ladies listened intently as Sara firstly explained yoga basics before trying gentle movement with controlled breathing.

A group of totally chilled ladies left the hall that night, many wishing to continue with further sessions.
