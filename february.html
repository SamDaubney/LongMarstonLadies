Sarah Grey / The 3 Easy Steps of Style

 

Sarah Grey / The 3 Easy Steps of Style

Long Marston Ladies started February 2022 in style, with Sarah Gray Stylist. Some members were easy to identify with classic, boho or edgy.

Everyone enjoyed the tips Sarah gave and will be sorting their wardrobes. Amongst the many things Sarah talked about was to be comfortable with your own style.

Everyone brought their everyday handbags and favourite shoes which confirmed their appearance style. Many laughs during the evening ensued as Sarah has a wonderful sense of humour.
